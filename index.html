The Lord's Prayer

Today, we will be praying with the Our Father. In Matthew's gospel, Jesus introduces the prayer with this instruction: "This is how you are to pray."Whether the Lord's Prayer is new to you or you have prayed it countless times without without thinking much about what you are saying, savor this opportunity to reflect deeply on the words, meaning, and source of this prayer.

-Jesus (Matthew 6: 9-13)
